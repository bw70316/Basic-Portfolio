About Me

As you have probably deciphered by now, my name is Brian Wisnowski and I have lived in Chapel Hill since I was in kindergarten. Naturally, I have grown to become a big fan of the Tar Heels, and I try to make it to at least one football and basketball game a season. I graduated Appalachian State University in 2009 with a degree in English. I tend to enjoy reading, playing poker, tennis, and racquetball, or at least I did before this coding class enveloped my life.

While I am musically inept myself, I do enjoy seeing live shows and try to go as often as I can. However, I fear this is another leisure activity that I will have to forego while I am in what can be aptly described as a coding cocoon. I would delve into the TV shows I like, such as The Simpsons, The X-Files, and more recently GOT and Fargo, but I have some more coding to do.
